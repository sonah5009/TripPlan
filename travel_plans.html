    • Relax on the sandy beach and soak up the sun.
    • Take a refreshing dip in the clear waters of the sea.

For a relaxing trip to the sea in Korea, I recommend the following itinerary:

Day 1: Busan - Morning: Start your day at Gwangalli Beach in Busan, known for its beautiful sandy shores and picturesque Gwangan Bridge. Enjoy a leisurely stroll along the beach and grab breakfast at one of the seaside cafes. - Afternoon: Head to Dokcheon-dong Youth Street, a vibrant area in Busan's Buk-gu district filled with trendy shops, boutiques, and eateries. Explore the unique street art and enjoy a delicious lunch at a local restaurant. - Evening: Spend the evening at Gwangalli Cafe Street, located near Gwangalli Beach. Relax at a seaside cafe, sipping on coffee or tea while enjoying stunning views of the beach and the bridge.

Day 2: Seoul - Morning: Travel to Seoul and start your day at Dongdaemun Fashion Town in the heart of the city. Explore the bustling fashion district, filled with shops selling the latest trends and unique designs. - Afternoon: Visit Euljiro Tile Market, a hidden gem in Seoul's Jung-gu district known for its colorful array of tiles and ceramics. Stroll through the market and pick up some unique souvenirs. - Evening: Conclude your trip at Guro Market Tteokbokki Alley in Seoul's Guro-gu district. Indulge in the famous Korean street food, tteokbokki (spicy rice cakes), and explore the lively market atmosphere.

This itinerary allows you to experience the sea in Busan on the first day and explore the vibrant city of Seoul on the second day. Enjoy your trip to the sea in Korea!
